Artists I Enjoy

Conan Grey

Conan Grey is a singer who started out on youtube. He has recently released his debut album Kid Krow. My favorites in the album is Heather, Fight or Flight, Cut that always bleeds. He has also released an EP "Sunset Season" which is also simply mindblowing.

J P Saxe

The first time I heard about J P Saxe was when I heard one of my friends sing 25 in Barcelona. I really loved it and started listening to it. One of his recent songs "If the world was ending" which is with his girlfriend, another amazing singer Julia Michaels is just simply amazing.

Finneas O'Connell

Finneas O'Connell is Billie Eilish's brother and an amazing music director. The first time I heard him singing is in the song Break My Heart Again, which is a slow and beautiful song. After which I heard Lets fall in love for the night which became my favorite very fast.
